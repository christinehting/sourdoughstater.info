Introduction

Like any other food dish, there are many different recipes and techniques available to make sourdough bread. This guide is just one version and was written to be beginner friendly. As you continue through your sourdough baking journey, I encourage you to branch out to other resources and to eventually develop your own recipe and set of techniques. A list of recommended articles and videos are provided on the resources page and should serve as a good jumping off point to dive into the fun and rewarding hobby of making sourdough.
